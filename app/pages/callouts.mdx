# Callouts

Callouts are intended to highlight some important message to the user.

They come in a wide variety of variants and can be configured with or without an icon.

<Callout variant="default" Icon={Link}>
  This is some kind of message.

  Some more text.
</Callout>

  Some more text.
</Callout>

</Callout>
